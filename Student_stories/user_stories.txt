USER STORIES FOR STUDENT

1. As as tudent, I want to submit my weekly internship log so that my supervisor and university can track my progress.
2. As a student, I want to view feedback from my supervisor so that I can improve my performance during the internship.
3. As a student, I want to see my evaluation scores so that I can understand how my internship performance is graded.
4. As a student, I want to update my internship placement details so that the university has accurate information about where I am working.
5. As a student, I want to view my internship progress dashboard so that I can monitor my submitted logs, evaluations, and overall performance.

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

1. The system shall allow students to submit weekly internship logs containing activities performed during the internship period.
2. The system shall allow supervisors to review submitted logs and provide feedback to students.
3. The system shall allow students to view supervisor feedback on their internship logs.
4. The system shall allow supervisors or the university to assign evaluation scores to students based on internship performance.
5. The system shall allow students to view their evaluation scores for transparency in grading.
6. The system shall allow students to update their internship placement details such as organization name, supervisor, and location.
7. The system shall provide a dashboard for students displaying internship progress including logs submitted, feedback received, and evaluation results.
8. The system shall maintain a record of all submitted internship logs and evaluations for tracking and reporting.

NON FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

1. Usability: The system interface shall be simple and easy for students and supervisors to navigate with minimal training.
2. Security: The system shall restrict access so that only authorized users (students, supervisors, administrators) can view or modify internship data.
3. Performance: The system shall load dashboards, logs, and feedback within an acceptable response time (e.g under 3 seconds)
4. Availability: The system shall be accessible online and available at least 99% of the time during internship periods.
5. Data Integrity: The system shall ensure submitted logs and evaluations are stored accurately without data loss.
6. Scalability: The system shall support multiple students and supervisors accessing the platform simultaneously.

WORKFLOW STATES

1. Placement Registered
The student records or updates their internship placement details (organization, supervisor, location).
2. Log Drafted
The student writes their weekly internship activities but has not yet submitted the log.
3. Log Submitted
The student submits the completed weekly log to the system for supervisor review.
4. Feedback Reviewed
The student views comments or feedback provided by the supervisor on the submitted log.
5. Evaluation Viewed
The student checks their internship evaluation scores assigned by the supervisor or university.
6. Progress Monitored
The student views their internship progress dashboard showing submitted logs, feedback, and performance status.

IDENTIFIED ENTITIES

Student – The user performing the internship and submitting logs.
Supervisor – The workplace or academic supervisor responsible for reviewing logs and giving feedback.
Internship Placement – Information about the organization where the student is placed.
Weekly Log – Records of tasks and activities performed during the internship each week.
Feedback – Comments provided by the supervisor on submitted logs.
Evaluation – Performance scores or grading assigned to the student.
Dashboard – Aggregated view of internship progress including logs, feedback, and scores.
